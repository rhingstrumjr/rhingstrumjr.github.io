The Problem

Almost all human conflict is caused by not understanding one another. My goal is to help communicate compassionate ideas.

My Story

For the past 8 years, I've been a teacher. I have seen the problems of miscommunication. Some have been between my students and me. Others have been between the administration and teachers. Sometimes the miscommunication is between myself and I!

What I've found comforting is writing down the thinking process. When I can see the trail of thoughts, I'm comforted. I want to build an app that helps people visualize their trail of thoughts. This has led me to take the route of a web developer.
